# 01 — Vision & Mission

## The heart behind it
The Daily Walk is a daily Christian newsletter that helps people strengthen their relationship with
Jesus in a way that feels **real, practical, and relatable to modern life**. It's meant to be
welcoming and encouraging for **both believers and non-believers** — easy to understand for people
at any stage of their faith journey. The north star: **help people find and follow Jesus.**

## What makes it different
- **Start with Jesus, not Genesis.** The Bible reading plan opens with who Jesus actually is, then
  what it means, then how it's lived out — so Scripture clicks before anyone hits the hard parts.
  (Inspired by Johnny Chang, @johnnychanglive — clear, direct, relevant to real life.)
- **Real, not religious.** Plain English, no jargon, no guilt. Honest prayer, practical application.
- **Hope built in.** A daily "Good News" briefing reminds readers that even when the world feels
  heavy, good is still happening and God is still moving.
- **A guided journey, personalized.** The Bible-in-a-Year experience starts on each person's own
  Day 1 so no one ever feels behind.

## Goals
- Grow a free subscriber base with a genuinely helpful daily email.
- Convert engaged readers to **Premium** (the guided Bible-in-a-Year) and **Patron** (mission/community).
- Build a welcoming community where people can ask questions, share, and grow together.
- Keep the core experience free and accessible for anyone who can't pay.
