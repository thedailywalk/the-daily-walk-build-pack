# 06 — Good News Section & Community

## Good News (daily section in the newsletter — free for all)
Each day, 3 short, **real, current, sourced** uplifting stories. Goal: a small daily reminder that
even when the world feels heavy, good is still happening and God is still moving.

**Each story includes:**
- A featured image (when available)
- A category pill (e.g., Generosity, Community, Restoration, Healing, Hope, Answered Prayer)
- A bold headline
- A 1–2 sentence plain-English summary (what happened + why it matters)
- A link to the original source

**Themes to rotate:** kindness, community, restoration, answered prayer, medical/scientific
breakthroughs, people helping others, positive global progress, hopeful moments in real life.

**Trusted sources to pull from:** Good News Network, Positive News, CBS "The Uplift", Good Good Good,
DailyGood. Always link and attribute.

**Build note:** model Good News stories as their own entity (title, summary, category, image URL,
source URL, date) so they can be curated/scheduled and reused. Consider an admin tool to add 3/day,
and optionally an automated draft step that fetches candidates for editor approval.
Layout reference: the "Good News" cards in `content-samples/landing-page-reference.html` and the
compact 3-up cards in `content-samples/sample-daily-issue.html`.

## Community
- A welcoming space (start with Discord or a simple members area; can grow later).
- **Free for every tier** — "no one should have to pay to belong."
- Patron extras live *inside* the community: a private Patron room, the Patron Prayer Wall, live
  prayer nights. Build community links into the site/portal; deep community features can be phase 2+.
