What a tier actually looks like

Two rhythms, one walk

The Daily Walk runs on two rhythms. The daily newsletter goes out to everyone on the calendar — encouragement, a prayer, Good News from around the world, the Wednesday Pastor's Take, and the Sunday Rest & Reflect. The Bible-in-a-Year journey is personal: it starts on your Day 1, the day you join, so you're never dropped into the middle and never feel behind before you begin.

  • Free — start walking with God daily. The daily encouragement, a real prayer, three Good News stories, the midweek Pastor's Take, and Sunday's reset. Everything you need to build the habit.
  • Premium — go through the Bible in a year, guided. Your own Day 1 to Day 365 track: each day's reading with a plain-English breakdown, a "What this shows us about God" reflection, real-life application, a reflection question and prayer prompt, an audio version, the Saturday deep-dive, a monthly workbook, and the full archive. Fall behind? Restart anytime.
  • Patron — help build the mission. Everything in Premium, plus closeness and a voice: a monthly Patron letter, live prayer nights, the Patron Prayer Wall, a private Patron room, a say in upcoming studies and Pastor's Take — and your support sponsors free access for someone who can't pay.

Frequently asked questions

Is the free version really free forever?
Yes. The daily devotional, a prayer, three Good News stories, the Wednesday Pastor's Take, the Sunday Rest & Reflect, and the community are all free with no card required. Premium adds the full guided Bible-in-a-Year journey; Patron adds deeper community and helps keep the free version free for everyone.
When does my Bible-in-a-Year plan start?
On your Day 1 — the day you join Premium. You're never dropped into the middle of someone else's calendar. If you'd rather follow along with the group, you can choose to join the community's current pace instead. Either way, you read Day 1, Day 2, Day 3 in order.
What if I fall behind on the reading?
No guilt, ever. You can restart your plan back at Day 1 or pick up right where you left off, anytime, from your account. The goal is 365 days in Scripture — not 365 days in a row.
Do I need to be a Christian to subscribe?
Not at all. The Daily Walk is written to be welcoming whether you've followed Jesus for decades, you're just curious, or you're somewhere in between. Start wherever you are.
What's the real difference between Premium and Patron?
Premium is the study path — it guides you through the whole Bible in a year, with plain-English breakdowns, real-life application, audio, and a monthly workbook. Patron is the mission & community tier: everything in Premium, plus a monthly Patron letter, live prayer nights, the Patron Prayer Wall, a private room, a say in what's next, and the chance to sponsor free access for someone who can't pay. Pick Premium to be guided; pick Patron to help build it.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account page in one click. You keep access through the end of your current billing period.
Can I upgrade or downgrade?
Yes. Change tiers anytime from your account page. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing period.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes. Full refund within the first 30 days, no questions asked. Just email hello@thedailywalk.com.
How fast do I get my first devotional after signing up?
The next scheduled send for your tier. Sign up tonight and you'll get tomorrow's Daily Devotional at 6:30 AM ET. Weekend sign-ups get the Sunday Rest & Reflect first.
Where does my money go?
Into keeping The Daily Walk running and free: writing, audio production, the community, and the printable resources. Patron support is what lets thousands of people read for nothing.
