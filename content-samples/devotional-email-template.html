Today's Reading

He walks willingly toward His own sacrifice for us.

Main: Leviticus 7–9 · Matthew 26 · Psalm 121

Matthew 26 begins the road to the cross — the last supper, Gethsemane, and Jesus' arrest. Even as everything turns against Him, He keeps moving forward, on purpose, for us.

He breaks the bread knowing exactly what's coming, and still says, "for you."
