# Reaching out to creators you'd love to feature

Warm, honest messages for asking Instagram creators/influencers whether you can
share or repost some of their content on **The Daily Walk** — and crediting them
well when you do.

## How to use these
- Replace anything in **[brackets]** with real, specific details. The more
  specific you are about *what* moved you, the more genuine it feels.
- Send it as an Instagram DM, or paste it into an email if you have one.
- Keep one promise above all: **never post their content until they say yes**,
  and only the specific posts they approve.

---

## When they say yes — lock it in kindly
A friendly reply *is* your record of permission, so keep the thread. Then ask:
> "Thank you so much — this means a lot! Two quick things so I represent you well:
> (1) How would you like to be credited (name + the exact @handle)?
> (2) Are there specific posts you'd love me to use, or any you'd rather I avoid?"

Save their answer with the post links you'll use.

## A few honest guardrails (so everyone's protected)
- **Only use what they personally approve**, and keep the credit on every share.
- A creator can give permission for their **own** photos/words. If a post uses
  someone else's photo, song, or art, they may not be able to hand that part on —
  when in doubt, ask, or use only the parts that are clearly theirs.
- If you ever can't reach someone, the safe move is to **link to their post**
  instead of reposting the content itself — a link always points people to them
  without copying anything.
- Keep it a relationship, not a transaction. A quick "thank you" when something
  goes live, and honoring any later request to remove it, builds trust that lasts.
